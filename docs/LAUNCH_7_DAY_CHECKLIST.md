# Maestro AI 7-Day Launch Checklist

A practical one-week launch sprint for sharing the live MVP and converting early users.

## Launch goals (7 days)

- Onboard 10-20 first users.
- Collect at least 10 high-quality feedback points.
- Capture 2-3 proof outcomes (time saved, workflow wins, or clarity gains).

## Day 1 - Launch announcement and first invites

- Post Founder Story on primary social channel.
- Send launch email to warm list.
- Send 10 direct invites (DM/SMS/WhatsApp).
- Share landing page in public launch posts: https://lrrecords.github.io/maestro-ai/
- Share live app only in direct early-user invites: https://maestro-ai.up.railway.app
- Use invite templates from `docs/LAUNCH_INVITE_TEMPLATES.md`.

Success criteria:

- 10 invites sent.
- 3+ users acknowledge and plan to test.

## Day 2 - Guided onboarding sessions

- Run 3-5 short guided walkthroughs (5-10 min each).
- Use runbook: `docs/LIVE_USER_ONBOARDING.md`.
- Capture friction points: login, navigation, first agent run, output clarity.

Success criteria:

- 3 completed sessions.
- Every session produces at least 1 actionable improvement note.

## Day 3 - Social proof and educational content

- Post one "build in public" update with a real screenshot.
- Post one short explanation: "what Maestro does / does not do".
- Publish one user-friendly thread/carousel about first-run workflow.

Success criteria:

- 1 proof-style post published.
- At least 3 meaningful comments/questions to respond to.

## Day 4 - Feedback-to-product loop

- Cluster feedback into themes: onboarding, trust, UX clarity, feature requests.
- Prioritize top 3 fixes or doc improvements.
- Ship at least one visible improvement.
- Post "we shipped this from your feedback" update.

Success criteria:

- Top 3 priorities documented.
- At least 1 improvement shipped publicly.

## Day 5 - Outreach expansion

- Send second-wave invites to adjacent contacts (managers, producers, studios).
- Share short demo clip or screenshot thread.
- Re-engage day-1 non-responders with follow-up nudge.

Success criteria:

- 10 additional invites sent.
- 3+ new users activate (login + first run).

## Day 6 - Case-study capture

- Identify 1-2 users with meaningful early outcomes.
- Capture mini case snippets:
  - Problem before Maestro
  - Workflow tested
  - Result/benefit observed
- Draft a short "results" post.

Success criteria:

- 2 reusable testimonial-style snippets prepared.

## Day 7 - Weekly review and next sprint plan

- Review metrics: invites sent, activations, completed onboarding calls, feedback count.
- Publish week-in-review post.
- Set next 7-day targets and content schedule.
- Update roadmap/backlog with validated requests.

Success criteria:

- Written week summary complete.
- Next sprint priorities committed.

## KPI tracker (simple)

Track daily in one note/spreadsheet:

- Invites sent
- Users activated (successful login)
- First workflow runs completed
- Guided calls completed
- Feedback items collected
- Improvements shipped
- Social/email responses

## Fast rules for launch week

- Use a 2-link CTA model: landing page for public posts, live app for direct invites.
- Keep onboarding friction low: separate token message, quick guided flow.
- Respond fast to early users (same day whenever possible).
- Convert feedback into visible changes quickly.
