# Mission Briefs Examples
---

This document provides example mission briefs for use in the CEO Command Centre "Fire Mission" feature. Copy and paste these into the dashboard or API as needed. Feel free to adapt them for your artists, releases, or campaigns.

---

**Note:** The dashboard only requires a Mission brief. There is no field for Release Title. To specify an artist, enter the artist_slug (e.g., `aria_velvet`) in the appropriate field.

## Minimal Mission Briefs (Quick Tests)

Use these for fast Fire Mission testing or when you need a simple, valid brief:

## Example 1: Release Campaign Launch

**Mission:**
> Launch the full marketing and distribution campaign for the upcoming single "Midnight Drive" by Luna Echo. Ensure all digital assets, press releases, and social media content are scheduled and approved. Coordinate with The Orchard for distribution and set up a pre-save campaign on Spotify.


## Example 1: Release Campaign Launch

**Mission:**
> Launch the full marketing and distribution campaign for the upcoming single "Midnight Drive" by Luna Echo. Ensure all digital assets, press releases, and social media content are scheduled and approved. Coordinate with The Orchard for distribution and set up a pre-save campaign on Spotify.

---

## Example 2: Social Media Blitz

**Mission:**
> Execute a 2-week social media blitz for the album "Neon Skies" by Atlas. Focus on Instagram Reels, TikTok challenges, and behind-the-scenes content. Collaborate with the artist for daily story updates and schedule influencer partnerships.

---

## Example 3: Press Outreach

**Mission:**
> Organize a press outreach campaign for "Golden Hour" by Sage. Prepare a press kit, draft email pitches, and target at least 20 music blogs and local radio stations. Track responses and schedule interviews as needed.

---

## Example 4: Tour Announcement

**Mission:**
> Announce the "Summer Lights" tour for Vinyl. Coordinate with venues, update the website, and create promotional graphics. Schedule email blasts and social posts for each city on the tour.

---

## Example 5: Fan Engagement Campaign

**Mission:**
> Launch a fan engagement campaign for Echo's upcoming EP. Set up a contest for fans to remix a track, promote on Discord and Twitter, and offer exclusive merch to winners. Collect user-generated content for future marketing.

---

## Example 6: Emergency Response

**Mission:**
> Respond to negative press regarding "Bridge" by Forge. Draft a public statement, coordinate with PR, and monitor social media for sentiment. Prepare talking points for interviews and update the FAQ on the website.

---

## Example 7: Data Analytics Review

**Mission:**
> Analyze the performance of "Atlas Rising" across all platforms. Prepare a report on streaming numbers, social engagement, and sales. Recommend adjustments to the ongoing marketing strategy based on findings.

---


## How to Use
- Copy the **Mission** field into the CEO Command Centre Fire Mission form.
- Enter the artist_slug (e.g., `aria_velvet`) in the artist field.
- Edit details as needed for your specific artist or campaign.
- For more ideas, see the [README.md](../README.md) or contribute your own briefs to this file.
